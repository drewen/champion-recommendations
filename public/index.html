How does this tool work?

We form a profile of you based off of your champion masteries and your most recent games. While champion roles don't directly influence this, different roles tend to be relegated to certain lanes and playstyles.
This profile is compared to Challenger tier players from all regions, as well as other users of this tool. Using this comparison, it suggests champions based off of weighing different parts of your profile more important than others.
